The Object

By day, a flawless mirror. By night, a universe.

Stellar Night is a two-way dielectric mirror with a secret. At rest it is simply a beautiful arched mirror — clean, deep, architectural. Activated, it dissolves into a recessed field of twinkling stars and luminous constellation traces.

Every star is mapped to its true position and colour. Summon the full northern sky, trace your zodiac, or recreate the exact heavens of a night that mattered to you.
